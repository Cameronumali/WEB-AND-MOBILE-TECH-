About Nyanga National Park

Nyanga, located in Zimbabwe's Eastern Highlands, offers:

  • Zimbabwe's highest mountain (Mount Nyangani)
  • Ancient stone ruins & rock art sites
  • Dramatic waterfalls including Mtarazi Falls
  • Lush tea estates and trout fishing

Key Attractions

Mtarazi Falls

Africa's second-highest waterfall at 762 meters

Nyanga Downs

Vast rolling hills perfect for hiking and horseback riding

Activities

  • Mountain climbing
  • Trout fishing in the Manyame River
  • Exploring ancient Ziwa ruins
  • Bird watching (over 300 species)

Planning Your Visit

Best time to visit: April to October (dry season)
